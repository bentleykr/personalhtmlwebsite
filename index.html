Hello Families! I have 21 years teaching experience, all in the same district. I received a BA in Elementary Education from the University of Kentucky, a MAEd in Instructional Leadership, all grades from EKU and am currently working on my second MAEd in School Librarian from EKU. I have taught 3rd grade, 4th grade, been an Assistant Principal, math intervention teacher and a Special Class Teacher in the areas of science lab, humanities, cultural studies, and career studies and financial literacy. I am looking forward to sharing this school year with you all!!! We are getting ready to have an exciting year together!
